Paperparrot is a native Mac client for Paperless-ng(x) written in Swift.

Features:
- Automatic assignment of custom attributes like tags, correspondents, creation date and document type
- Add documents in various formats like PDF, JPEG, PNG or TXT
- Scan documents from right within Paperparrot using your phones camera or an Airscan-capable network scanner
- Secure your documents using Touch-ID
- Extensive search capabilities
- Create custom views to easily access sets of documents
- Share documents to Paperparrot from other apps
- Synchronize documents from your Paperless-ng(x) server to your device for offline access
- Dark mode!
